Reflection Analysis

The Principles of Marketing module has been a very important part of my journey as it has contributed to the development of my perception of professional development and employability skills. Although at first, I was not sure about group activities, with practice and feedback, I have gained some confident communication skills that allow me to express ideas clearly when making presentations and discussing topics in the classroom.

Timekeeping has been a part of my educational identity. A 100% attendance has not only proven to be reliable to my lecturers but also helped me to develop the necessary level of discipline necessary in a professional setting. This sense of timeliness is a manifestation of my knowledge that credibility is established by little bites.

Working together has helped me to appreciate different ideas. Collaborating with colleagues of diverse backgrounds has helped me become more culturally aware and more skilled to make a valuable contribution towards team goals. I have also come to understand that effective teamwork necessitates active listening, flexibility and sincerity towards the idea of others.

Self-directed learning has been revolutionizing. Assuming responsibility of my Canvas preparation and taking initiative in other resources has boosted my academic development. This self-managed style will be of good use in my future life when I will be in the professional sphere and should be able to learn constantly to remain competitive.

In the future, I will continue to work on these competencies. I aspire to be in a management role where I will be able to put these skills to use in order to bring about organisational success as well as continue to develop as a professional.
